Kenetic & The Dagda

What once started as a student job in the youth centre later grew into a small career as a DJ. Motivated in the beginning by free beer, that later grew into a mild obsession wuth music. This led to me performing to crowds ranging from fifty to about thousand people.

Experiments like adding extra samples, sets with 3 cd-players and dj battles grew from this organically. There was a period you could find me nearly every weekend in some dark and sweaty room providing music. One of my personal highlights was winning a national competition to play as only one of three Belgian acts on a festival near Ghent. I also have fond memories of times where I got to play on the same stage as some of my favourite acts, and the compliments I got on my technical skills by these producers.
