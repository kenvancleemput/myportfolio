Psywaves

When I was young, I also got involved in party management. This ranged from small parties in Zazou, a small club in Lier, to larger gatherings in Hangar 26 in Antwerp. I was also a known face in the Belgian party scene, often aiding with building up and breaking down, and earned the trust of many organisations so that I was allowed to be the man at the ticket booth.

I grew out of it when I started my career as an accountant, both because my musical taste evolved and combining party organiser with a full time accountant career wasn't conducive to either. I do, however, still look back with pride at the events I've organised, and the lessons I've learned in that time organising events from 50 to 600 people.
