Build-A-City

During project management we split up into groups of 5 to design an island for our benevolent dictator aka our teacher.This was to give us an insight into SCRUM and the methods involved. We ran through three sprints, and during each sprint each group was assigned products from the product backlog to realize.

My team was tasked with designing the beach bar, chippy and wellness center. Due to the time pressure involved, it was heartening to see trust grow each sprint. I can see how this would lead to a good work environment in a real workplace.
