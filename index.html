Welcome to my page.

Thank you for visiting my site. Here you can find all the relevant information about me, my projects, the things I've learned in school, and the direction I would like to take in the future. The reason for this page is twofold: first of all, it's a requirement of my degree. All the students need to make a site about themselves, which naturally segues into the second (and more important) reason: this is my calling card to future employers. After all, programming isn't just writing code. It's also about web development and databases, and I hope that my website can interest you, the reader, and by extension your company, to get in touch with me about interesting job opportunities.

How is this site structured?
